Pancake Breakfast TACOS

Ingredients:

  • pancake mix
  • milk
  • eggs
  • bacon
  • genuine maple syrup
  • butter

Method:

  1. mix the eggs and milk with the panncake mix until it is soft enough to pour
  2. pour the mix over a buttered pan set to medium to high heat
  3. cook until both sides of the pancake are light brown
  4. repeat step 3 with the bacon
  5. scramble or fry some more eggs
  6. (optional) add cheese to the pancake
  7. place the cooked eggs and bacon on top of the pancake
  8. pour maple syrup on the taco
  9. fold the pancke into the shape of a taco
  10. ENJOY!
